Understanding Soil Types

Sandy Soil

Light, warm, dry, and low in nutrients. Perfect for root vegetables.

Loamy Soil

Perfect balance of sand, silt, and clay. Ideal for most crops.

Peaty Soil

High in organic matter, retains moisture. Great for acid-loving plants.

Clay Soil

Heavy, cold, and wet in winter. Good for summer crops.
